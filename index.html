About the Event

More Than a
Tournament.

Will was an avid disc golfer who was just beginning to work on his game when his life was cut way too short at 20 years old. An auto mechanic by trade, he loved cars as much as he loved people. After a short-term missions trip to Spain with his young adult group, Will was inspired to one day use his skills to serve missionaries in need — he came home with a heart to help people with their cars around the world. The Will Miles Memorial Disc Golf Tournament honors that spirit: a love of the game, a love of community, and a desire to make a difference.

Now in its 2nd year, this tournament is growing into a premier event on the local disc golf calendar. Players of all skill levels are welcome to compete, connect, and contribute to a cause that matters.
