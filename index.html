About the Event

More Than a
Tournament.

The Will Miles Memorial Disc Golf Tournament is a charity event that brings together the disc golf community to celebrate a beloved friend, competitor, and champion — while raising funds that make a real difference in our community.

Now in its 2nd year, this tournament has grown into a premier event on the local disc golf calendar. Players of all skill levels are welcome to compete, connect, and contribute to a cause that matters.

Cathedral Land & Speed Co.

Also known as STEAM Cathedral

A 501(c)(3) nonprofit using motorsports and automotive arts to teach STEAM education to underserved youth — hands-on, collaborative, and real.

Through karting, fabrication, welding, mechanics, and artistic mediums, Cathedral Land & Speed Co. builds hard skills, soft skills, and confidence that opens doors to workplace opportunities for the next generation.
